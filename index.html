At Food Court Everybody Eats

Located inside the McKinley Mall, Food Court Skatepark will be a climate controlled indoor facility that will provide an amazing skate experience to the WNY area. The facility will be open from 12pm to 9pm every day with special hours for certain special events and sessions. It will host an array of both skate and non-skate events that will be fun for the entire family, as well as all walks of life. When you're here, we believe that every person should feel comfortable and at home because at Food Court...EVERYBODY EATS!

About

Food Court Skatepark will offer a little bit of everything for everyone. For beginners, we will offer group classes as well as private lessons. For those advanced skaters, we will offer a variety of contests with awesome prizes. Food Court will be a space where people from all walks of life can get together and enjoy themselves. We'll host special events that bring pro skaters and teams to the park as well as linking up artists and athletes for a completely unique and memorable experience. Parents and spectators will have a designated, safe viewing area out of the action so you can observe everything that's going on without having to worry about getting caught up in the action.

Why should we purchase now instead of when you open?

Purchasing goods now helps us get working capital to address any issues we may have before the park is open and able to generate its own revenue. Also, successful indigogo campaigns draw attention from brands and boost the chances that they would come visit! Not to mention you're saving a TON on each item!

OF COURSE... Sharing is caring right? Any of our digital perks can be transferred to someone else. When the time comes, just shoot us an email at whatsup@foodcourtskatepark.com or stop by in person and we'll take care of it for you.
