What Is An Annual Membership? Do I Need One For Admission?

An Annual Membership is a one time yearly payment that gets you:

-12 free sessions to use whenever you want within the year

-Access to our Members Only events within the park

-20% off other sessions and passes

An Annual Membership is NOT required for general admission, you just miss out on free sessions, exclusive events and park discounts.
